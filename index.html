About me

Pham Ba Cong

Hello, my name is Cong, and I am an aspiring self-taught musician. I have taught myself the guitar, bass, as well as music recording and production. While I am mainly a rock player, I constantly look to expand my horizon to other genres and instruments. Here are some of my works, from tracks I performed and produced with Hanoi - Ams Rock Club, to some of my solo projects.
